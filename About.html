Our Journey To Fridgi
Our journey began as a group of four passionate students at the Holon Institute of Technology (HIT): Reem, Rotem, Asaf, and Daniel. Under the mentorship of our lecturer, Yossi, we set out to solve a common problem—how to make grocery management and meal planning more efficient and less stressful. We realized that keeping track of what's in the kitchen and planning meals around available ingredients could be a challenge. This inspired us to create Fridgi, a smart kitchen inventory manager that leverages AI to simplify these tasks. Fridgi doesn't just track your groceries; it also helps you plan meals and ensures you never miss an essential item. Through teamwork, creativity, and countless hours of development, we turned our idea into a reality. Fridgi represents our dedication to making everyday life easier, and we’re proud to share our creation with you.
